# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Employees use the Counter on mobile and tablet during active booth shifts where speed, clarity, and reliable offline behavior matter. Admins use the same product to manage booths, schedules, products, sales, receipts, and employee access without losing confidence in live operations.

## Product Purpose
Mini's Pastries POS exists to let booth staff record sales quickly at the counter, keep working through unstable connectivity, and give admins clear operational control over inventory, scheduling, reporting, and corrections. Success looks like fast sale flow, trustworthy offline recovery, and admin tools that stay out of the way until they are needed.

## Brand Personality
Friendly, reliable, efficient. The interface should feel welcoming and easy to use on touch devices while still reading as a serious operational tool for a live business.

## Anti-references
Not playful to the point of feeling toy-like. Not overly component-heavy with stacks of boxes and panels competing for attention. Not overengineered in either the visual language or the workflows.

## Design Principles
1. Counter speed wins over ornament.
2. Optimistic feedback should keep operators moving without hiding source-of-truth state.
3. Mobile and tablet use are first-class, not reduced desktop layouts.
4. Admin control should feel clear and dependable, not dense or intimidating.
5. Shared patterns should reduce UI noise instead of multiplying screens and components.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast and interaction standards as the default baseline. Support reduced motion, strong touch targets for tablet and mobile use, clear focus states, and plain feedback that remains understandable during fast-paced counter work.
